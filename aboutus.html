About Us

We are SafeBoda. Your city ride

SafeBoda is a rapidly growing consumer platform that is revolutionizing transportation, payments and on-demand services in Africa.

It is a community of enterpreneurs and boda riders working together to improve professional standards across the urban transortation industry in Africa. Our aim is to improve the industry for both drivers and passengers by increasing the number of safe trips taken per day and by making travel around cities convenient and stress free.
